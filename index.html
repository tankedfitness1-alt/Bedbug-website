Meet BEDBUG

BEDBUG is a one-of-a-kind VW Beetle camper with vintage style, modern adventure energy and a build story worth showing off. From workshop floors to coastal escapes, this little Beetle has been transformed into a head-turning camper made for weekends away, coffee stops, beach days and sunset camp setups.

The Build Journey

2021 — The Idea Begins

The vision started with a classic VW Beetle and a wild idea: turn it into a tiny camper with huge personality.

Workshop Days

Panels, paint, fit-out, wiring, interior work and the rear kitchen all came together piece by piece.

Rear Kitchen Complete

The camper setup came alive with a clean rear kitchen, storage, sink, microwave and coffee-ready bench space.

First Trip Ready

After years of building, BEDBUG is ready for coastal drives, hinterland getaways and its first proper adventure.
