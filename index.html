Meet BEDBUG

BEDBUG is a custom VW Beetle camper with vintage character, beachside energy and a build story worth showing off. From workshop floors to coastal getaways, this little Beetle has been transformed into a head-turning camper made for weekend escapes, coffee stops, sunset setups and road trip memories.

The Build Journey

2021 — The Start of the Build

The vision started with a classic Beetle shell and a wild idea: create a tiny camper with huge personality.

Workshop Days

The structure, body, paint, wiring and camper fit-out all came together piece by piece inside the workshop.

Rear Kitchen Complete

BEDBUG came alive with a rear kitchen setup built for coffee, quick meals, storage and campsite mornings.

Ready for the First Trip

After years of work, BEDBUG is ready for Noosa, Byron Bay, hinterland drives and coastal adventures.
