The BEDBUG Story

From an old rundown VW Beetle to a one-of-a-kind retro camper built for unforgettable adventures.

Where It Started

BEDBUG began life as an old VW Beetle with a dream far bigger than the car itself.

The vision was simple: build something completely unique that combined classic Volkswagen styling, modern reliability and the freedom of weekend adventure.

What followed was a five-year passion project that transformed a tired Beetle into one of Australia's most unique retro camper experiences.

Modern Mechanical Upgrades

Subaru 2.0L Boxer Engine

Modern power, improved reliability and a driving experience that makes every road trip enjoyable.

Performance Brakes

Upgraded braking system designed for confidence and safety on long drives.

Coil Over Rear Suspension

Improved ride quality and handling while maintaining classic Beetle character.

Life Inside BEDBUG

BEDBUG isn't just a vehicle — it's a tiny adventure home.

Every detail has been designed to make weekend escapes comfortable, simple and memorable.
