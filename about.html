About BEDBUG

From an old rundown VW Beetle to a one-of-a-kind retro camper, BEDBUG was built to bring classic style and weekend adventure together.

The Build

BEDBUG started life as an old rundown VW Beetle before being transformed into a custom camper with a completely unique personality.

The build blends vintage VW charm with modern upgrades, making it more reliable, more capable and far more comfortable for weekend escapes.

Modern Mechanical Updates

  • Subaru 2.0L boxer engine conversion
  • Upgraded performance brakes
  • Coil-over rear suspension
  • Classic VW styling with improved drivability

Camper Setup

  • Spacious double bed
  • Plenty of luggage storage
  • Reading lights
  • Rear kitchen setup
  • Microwave
  • Coffee machine
  • Sink with running water
  • 65L fridge

Rear Kitchen Living

The rear kitchen is built for real weekend use. Open it up, make a coffee, grab breakfast, wash up with running water and keep everything cold in the 65L fridge.

It turns every stop into a proper little camp setup.
